Amazon's global headquarters are located in Seattle, Washington, at 410 Terry Avenue North.

  • The company occupies more than 40 owned and leased buildings throughout Seattle's South Lake Union and Denny Triangle neighborhoods.

  • In addition to its Seattle headquarters, Amazon is developing a second headquarters in the National Landing neighborhood of Arlington, Virginia.

  • This new campus will feature energy-efficient offices, retail spaces, and public green areas.

  • Amazon also has a significant presence in India, with Amazon Seller Services Pvt Ltd operating within the country.
